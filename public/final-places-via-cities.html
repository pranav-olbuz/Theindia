About Gujarat

Gujarat is located at the western part of India and famous for its Food, Gir lions, and urban areas like Surat, Ahmedabad and Gandhinagar. Gujarat has very rich culture and ancient history which attracts tourists from all around the world. Gujarat has its own reputation in world because of their Economy, Industrial growth, Education system, High education institutes like IIM, Governance & administration, Agriculture, Food, power plants and many other things too. From the last decade or so Gujarat has invested so much time, money and resources to its tourism department and its worth for the state. Every year Gujarat and Gujarati people celebrates festivals like Navratri & Uttarayan all over the world. Navratri is actually 9 night long festival where people wears traditional cloths and enjoys Garba with friends or relatives on some Dhinchaak music.

Gujarat - The cultural place in India Called as "Different people with one soul"

If you want to look something out of box, with beauty, culture and many more then you must visit Gujarat. Can say all part have its own uniqueness with beauty in culture, food, festivals & so on. Starting from weekend getaways, Beaches, Ecotourism, Heritage, Gandhi Circuit, Museums, Religious sites, Fairs & Festivals, Restaurants & Handicrafts; there are many more places in Gujarat with tons of emotions. There are historic places with cultural moments and multi cuisines. Gujarat offers traveler or a holiday maker desires to see and enjoy. To say in the one word, everything in Gujarat is just awesome starting from thinking till human being.

Weekend Getaways

Wonderful experience you feel in Gujarat with its inner beauty. With religious places like Ambaji, Bhavnagar, Bhuj, Champaner-Pavagadh, Dakor, Dholavira, Dwarka with one of the amazed experience of Bird Sanctuary in Thol Lake. Rann of Kutch is one of the admirable places with its white beauty & Indian Bustard sanctuary, Mandvi Beach for its natural water. Famous nature education sanctuary located at Hingolgadh is one of the best places to visit. Don't forget to add Polo Monument and vijaynagar forest and Nalsarovar Bird Sanctuary in your package whenever you plan your Gujarat tours.

Heritage

There are many heritage sites located in Gujarat and you can experience the same while going for small heritage walk in Ahmedabad. The walk is organized by Ahmedabad Municipal Corporation starts from Kalupur Swaminarayan temple to Juma Mosque every day. Full of attractions on the side of heritage covers the Archaeology, Architecture, Buddhist Footprints, Fort, Gravesite, Haveli, and Palace, oldest memory from stepwell, World Heritage collection and Heritage walk as memory in Gujarat heart which is live.
